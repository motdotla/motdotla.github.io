About Me

Hey there, my name is Scott Motte. You can call me Mot, for short.

I am a software developer from Perris, CA, now living in Los Angeles, CA. I love to code, specialize in Ruby, and have a talent for product.

I'm self-taught and creative. I can ship code with the best of them. I'm continually learning and believe that it is not how good you are but how good you want to be, and at the moment I'm taking the Stanford Machine Learning course.

Check out my code on GitHub, take a peak at my resume, or reach out to me at mot@mot.la. I'd love to hear from you!
