Possessions

I'm taking inventory of my material possessions. While I would not say these things define me, they definitely are a reflection of me.

These are not the majority of my possessions. In fact, they are the minority. The majority of my items I did not find worth listing! And I would bet you feel the same way!

Making a list like this helps you realize, a large portion of your possessions are not valuables [1]. There is wisdom in that. Likely, you will be a little bit wiser with your purchases next time.
