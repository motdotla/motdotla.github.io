Organizer for CodeDay LA url

CodeDay is a nationwide event where students programmers get together to build apps and games in 24 hours. It's a hackathon with a student focus.

I worked with Zach Latta and Brandon Ramirez to organize the winter 2014 CodeDay Los Angeles.

For my part I helped secure the location, raise sponsorship, and help Zach emcee the event. It was a nice event with a lot of first timers to a hackathon. There were great apps and hacks created (like an electric saxaphone!). NPR even showed up to interview the student hackers.
