Pixel is a chat-based application for printing photos directly from your phone.

I invented Pixel because my wife and I were finding it time consuming to get real photos printed. Pixel is unique in that you just text your photo to Pixel, and a couple days later it arrives at your doorstep. It is great because it takes less than 5 minutes of your time!

Technologies include:

  • Ruby
  • Ruby on Rails
  • Stripe Payment Processing
  • RESTful APIs
  • Twilio
  • Illustrations
