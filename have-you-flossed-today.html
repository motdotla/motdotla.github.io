Have you flossed today? url

I was terrible at flossing. My dentist consistently scolded me. In response, I built Flossed Today. It sent you a text message each day, reminding you to floss. You could also track your progress by responding 'yes' or 'no' to each reminder. Thanks to the website I actually started flossing and so did others!

The website employed a combination of technologies such as:

  • Merb
  • Sending of SMS messages through GMail
  • Receiving/Parsing of SMS messages with mms2r
  • Google Charts API

Flossed Today launched March 2009. Thoughtbot tweeted about it.

On February 4th, 2012 I shut down the website.
