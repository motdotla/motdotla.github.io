Dotenv (NodeJS) url

I built dotenv one afternoon for my own use. I was building lots of NodeJS projects at the time and found nconf bulky and unweildy for my taste. Dotenv loads configuration via a simple .env file.

I continue to maintain the library. Additionally, I opted to bring in other contributors and give them full admin access. That has gone a long way to keeping things maintained and taught me some things about shared ownership.

Dotenv has since grown into a very popular library with hundreds of thousands of downloads a month.
