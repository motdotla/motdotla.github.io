CodeCampus is a computer science training website for elementary teachers. It provides training and complete lesson plans for teachers to implement coding lessons into their elementary curriculum.

I was contracted to build the subscription platform for the lesson plans and training videos. I worked closely with the founder to implement authentication, advanced authorization via tiered membership layers, and payment processing. I also included an administrative backend so CodeCampus' support team could better serve it's users.

Technologies include:

  • Ruby
  • Ruby on Rails
  • Stripe Payment Processing
  • RESTful APIs
  • JavaScript
